【Script installation】

1. Copy the ReTrack.jsxbin file to the AE script directory:

Win: ...Adobe After Effects CC\Support Files\Scripts\ScriptUI Panels\
Mac: Applications\Adobe After Effects CC\Scripts\ScriptUI Panels\


2. Open the AE software settings, check *Allow script to read and write and access the network:
Win: Edit - Preferences - General
Mac: After Effects CC - Preferences - General

AE CC2019.1 or later at:
Win: Edit - Preferences - Scripts and Expressions
Mac: After Effects CC - Preferences - Scripts and Expressions


3. You can see the ReTrack.jsxbin script under the window menu at the top of the software
