[Script installation]

1. Copy the Magic Switcher.jsxbin file to the AE script directory:

Win: ...Adobe After Effects CC\Support Files\Scripts\ScriptUI Panels\
Mac: Applications\Adobe After Effects CC\Scripts\ScriptUI Panels\


2. Open the AE software settings, check * to allow scripts to read and write and access the network:
Win: Edit-Preferences-General
Mac: After Effects CC-Preferences-General

AE CC2019.1 or higher is in:
Win: Edit-Preferences-Scripts and Expressions
Mac: After Effects CC-Preferences-Scripts and Expressions


3. You can see the Magic Switcher.jsxbin script under the window menu at the top of the software
