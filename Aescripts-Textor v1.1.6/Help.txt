 INSTALLATION

(1) copy the file Textor.jsxbin into the folder
"Support Files/Scripts/ScriptUI Panels" (on Windows) or
"Scripts/ScriptUI Panels" (on Mac) of your After Effects
installation. 

(2) Start Textor.jsxbin (via the "Window" menu in After Effects)



 УСТАНОВКА

(1) скопируйте файл Textor.jsxbin в папку
"Support Files/Scripts/ScriptUI Panels" (на Windows) или
"Scripts/ScriptUI Panels" (на Mac) вашего After Effects. 

(2) Запустите Textor.jsxbin (через меню "Window" в After Effects).
